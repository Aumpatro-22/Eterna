AI and Model Training

In beta, we may use submitted content and usage data to train and improve our AI systems (e.g., story/imagery generation and recommendations). We apply technical and organizational safeguards to reduce risks and respect memorial contexts.

If you prefer your content not be used for future model training, contact us and we’ll apply an opt-out to new training runs. Note: content used in prior training may not be fully removable from existing models, but we will exclude it from future training to the extent feasible.

We retain data as long as needed to provide the service and for legitimate interests (security, compliance). You can request deletion of your account and content. Training data already incorporated into models may not be fully extractable; however, we will exclude your content from future training when you opt out.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No system is 100% secure; please use strong, unique passwords.

Data may be processed in locations where we or our providers operate. We take steps to protect data according to this Policy regardless of location.
